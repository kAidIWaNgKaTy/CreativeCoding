BORN IN MAGMA

Scientific Name:

The name of my
creature is Sm0ke

Discovered by/at:

Katy on Tuesday Oct 15

Family:

Produced by volcano eruption. Sm0ke comes to live with many siblings inside one volcano. Their lifespan ranges from a few months to 5 years. They don't reproduce its own kind but their parent, the volcano does.

Habitat:

  • Magma
  • Volcano

    • Appearance:

    Sm0ke looks like black/grey smoke in almost a sphere shape, with no fixed shape and edge, constantly changing its size.

    Key Characteristics:

    • Introverted to human
    • Responsive to the volcano
    • Free spirit

    What we know so far about this creature: Sm0ke likes flowing around in its habitat which is the volcano. Whenever the human tries to approach it, it avoids human touch. However, when hearing the sound from the volcano, it gets excited and changes shape and size.

    How can we interact with this creature: We can observe its motion and changes according to the sound of the volcano; We can also wear heat-resistant gloves and try to touch it.
